# QA Scoring Dimensions

Six dimensions, each scoring 0-2. Maximum total: 12.

---

## 1. Correct routing (0-2)

**2:** Routed correctly with the right team.
**1:** Acceptable but not optimal.
**0:** Wrong team, or should have escalated and did not.

Always scores 0 if not escalated: ban appeal, account compromise, fraud report, churn risk, third+ contact.

---

## 2. Information collection (0-2)

**2:** All required fields collected. No unnecessary asks.
**1:** Most fields collected, or one field missed.
**0:** Key information missing that would prevent resolution.

Required fields: payment issue (player ID, transaction ID, platform), refund (player ID, transaction ID, purchase date), account access (player ID, login method), bug report (player ID, device, OS version).

---

## 3. Tone appropriateness (0-2)

**2:** Tone matched the player's emotional state.
**1:** Acceptable but missed an opportunity to acknowledge distress.
**0:** Tone was wrong for the situation.

Always scores 0: telling an angry player to calm down, using "as per my previous message" with a frustrated player, jumping straight to troubleshooting with a distressed player.

---

## 4. Scope compliance (0-2)

**2:** Stayed within T1 scope.
**0:** Agent made an unauthorised commitment or promised a specific outcome.

Always scores 0: promising a refund, stating a ban will be lifted, interpreting TOS from memory.

---

## 5. Accuracy (0-2)

**2:** All information accurate and KB-backed.
**1:** Language suggests policy stated without KB backing.
**0:** Factually incorrect information.

---

## 6. Player experience (0-2)

**2:** Clear, concise, clear next step given.
**1:** Acceptable but unclear on next steps or overly long.
**0:** Would increase the player's frustration.

---

## Score interpretation

| Total | Assessment |
|---|---|
| 11-12 | Excellent |
| 9-10 | Good |
| 7-8 | Acceptable |
| 5-6 | Below standard |
| 0-4 | Failing |
